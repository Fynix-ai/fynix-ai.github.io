FAQs for Fynix Copilot/Code Assistant

Product

  • What is Fynix Code Assistant? Fynix Code Assistant is an AI-powered code assistant that helps developers accelerate their workflows by providing real-time code suggestions, flow diagrams, and AI-driven reviews directly in IDEs.
  • What programming languages does Fynix support? Fynix supports a wide range of programming languages, including Python, JavaScript, Java, C++, Go, and more. We aim to continuously expand support for additional languages based on developer needs.
  • What IDEs are compatible with Fynix? Currently, Fynix integrates seamlessly with VSCode. We are working to add support for other popular IDEs like IntelliJ in upcoming updates.
  • How does Fynix ensure the accuracy of its suggestions? Fynix uses state-of-the-art AI models and continuously learns from user interactions to improve its suggestions and provide the most relevant insights.
  • How often are new features and updates released? Our development team releases regular updates to improve performance, add new features, and expand IDE compatibility. Keep an eye on our release notes for details about the latest enhancements.
  • Does Fynix Copilot store my code? No, Fynix ensures complete code privacy. We don’t store or share your code, and our models operate with zero data retention to guarantee your data’s security.
  • How does Fynix protect sensitive data? Fynix follows strict data security protocols and complies with industry standards to ensure the safety of sensitive information. All communications are encrypted end-to-end.
  • Does Fynix integrate with third-party tools securely? Yes, Fynix uses secure APIs and OAuth for third-party integrations, ensuring the protection of your data and credentials.
